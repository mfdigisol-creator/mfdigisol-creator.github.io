These guidelines explain how enquiries and catalogue information are handled. Final order-specific terms are confirmed with our team on official WhatsApp before payment.

Ordering

Select a product and use its WhatsApp button. Our team will confirm the product code, current availability, final payable amount, payment instructions and delivery arrangements before an order is accepted.

Prices

Calculated retail prices are displayed where our automated catalogue can confidently classify a design. If classification is uncertain or a supplier-price change requires review, the website displays “Price on enquiry” instead of estimating a price.

Availability

“Available to order” reflects the latest synchronized availability from our approved supplier source. Updates run approximately every 12 hours. Availability can change between updates, so confirmation from our team is required before payment.

Payment & delivery

Orders are fulfilled by Cash on Delivery within Pakistan, normally through TCS or Leopards Courier. Delivery costs Rs. 300 within Sialkot and Rs. 600 outside Sialkot for parcels up to 1 kg. Charges may increase depending on parcel weight or volume and will be communicated during the confirmation call. Estimated delivery TAT is up to 7 days after order confirmation and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances.

Cancellation

To cancel an order before the confirmation call, contact us on official WhatsApp at +92 321 6115731 and share the relevant order details.

Exchange & returns

Eligibility depends on the product condition, the nature of the concern and the terms confirmed for the order. Please inspect your parcel promptly and contact our team with the product code and supporting photographs before returning anything. Do not send a return without confirmation.

Catalogue information

Product images, information, codes, prices and availability from the relevant Dawood Designers listings are used and synchronized with written authorization. Product colours may appear differently because of photography, lighting and screen settings.

Privacy

Information voluntarily provided through WhatsApp, email or the website enquiry flow is used to answer enquiries and process requested services. Please do not submit sensitive financial credentials through website forms.
